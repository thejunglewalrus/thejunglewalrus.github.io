Gambling

Bet formats: 100 raw, 1,000 with commas, 1k/1m/1b denominations, 50%/.5% percent of balance, 1/2 fraction, word fractions like half/quarter/tenth, or all/everything. Minimum bet across all gambling commands is 100 walrus martinis. The broadcaster can open or close the casino with !walrusino open|close — while closed, every gambling command refuses.

Broadcaster-only

Restricted to the broadcaster. Mods do NOT have access — these mint or overwrite balances arbitrarily, or gate the gambling room.

!walrusino open|close Open or close the gambling room. When closed, all gambling commands refuse with a one-line "casino closed" notice. State persists across bot restarts.
